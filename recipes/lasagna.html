Lasagna

this is lasagna. one of my favorite dishes to eat. maybe not to make because it has it's complicated steps to it but the final result always makes it worth it... The truth about it is that I have never made lasagna in my life but I sure as hell enjoy eating it.

ingredients
  • olive oil
  • ground beed
  • onion
  • bell pepper
  • garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • oregano
  • parskey
cooking steps and more
  1. put pasta in water to boil
  2. brown the ground beef
  3. cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic and add back the beef
  4. transfer to medium pot
  5. boil and the drain lasagna noodles
  6. assemble lasagne
  7. bake
  8. cool and serve
